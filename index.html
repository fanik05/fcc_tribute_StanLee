Stan Lee

With great power comes great responsibility

Here's a time line of Stan Lee's life:

  • 1922 - Stanley Martin Liber, better known as Stan Lee, was born on December 28 in Manhattan. Son of Celia and Jack Liber, a marriage of Romanian Jewish immigrants. His father was a tailor and had a younger brother called Larry Lieber.
  • 1940 - After completing compulsory secondary education at a bronx institute, he worked as a messenger, writer of obituaries and press releases, until shortly after he got an assistant job at Timely Comics, which is currently MARVEL comics.
  • 1944 - He debuted as a screenwriter at age 20 in a 2-page story starring Captain America.
  • 1947 - He married Joan Boocock Lee who died unfortunately in 2017.
  • 1953 - His second son named Jan Lee was born.
  • 1960 - He was about to leave marvel comics and a year later he created the first family of superheroes in marvel: the fantastic four
  • 1967 - Stan Lee's favorite character is spider-man, created by Steve Didko and also made a cameo in the spider-man series.
  • 2008 - His first cameo in a movie is the first time he appeared in a movie starring one of his characters was in iron man.
  • 2018 - He died by neumonia on 12 November in California.
